Your Privacy & Security Matter

At Voxity, we believe that accessibility and privacy go hand in hand. Hearable is designed with strong privacy safeguards to protect your communication and personal data.

Local Storage Only

All transcripts and sensitive data are stored locally on your device, not on external servers. Your conversations stay private.

DPDP Act Compliant

We fully comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 for all aspects of data handling and user privacy.

Transparent Policies

Clear privacy policy and terms of service explaining how your data is used and stored, with no hidden clauses or complicated language.

Complete Data Control

You have complete control over your data with easy options to delete or manage your information at any time.

Your Questions, Answered

Get answers to common questions about Hearable and how it can help you.

Do I need the internet to use Hearable?

Hearable works best with an internet connection for full functionality. However, we've designed core features to work with limited connectivity, and some basic functionalities are available offline.

What languages are supported?

Hearable currently supports 4 Indian languages: Hindi, English, Tamil, and Telugu. We're continuously working to add more languages and dialects based on user feedback.

Is Hearable free for students or NGOs?

Yes, Hearable is available free of charge for students with hearing impairments and registered NGOs working with the deaf and hard-of-hearing community. Please contact us for details on our accessibility program.

Can it help during doctor visits or interviews?

Absolutely! Hearable is designed to support critical communication scenarios like medical appointments, job interviews, educational settings, and government services. The app includes specialized vocabulary for healthcare and professional contexts.

How accurate is the transcription?

Our speech recognition technology achieves over 90% accuracy for most Indian languages and accents. The personal dictionary feature allows you to add custom words and names to improve accuracy for your specific needs.

Can I use my existing phone number?

Yes, Hearable works with your existing phone number. There's no need to get a new number or make any changes to your current phone service.
