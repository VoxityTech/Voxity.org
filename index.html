Core Features

Hearable provides a comprehensive set of accessibility features designed for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community in India.

Real-time Transcription

Convert speech to text and text to speech during phone calls for seamless communication.

Keep Your Number

Use your existing phone number with Hearable—no need to switch to a new number.

Save Transcripts

Store call transcripts locally on your device for privacy and future reference.

Personal Dictionary

Add custom words, names, and acronyms to improve transcription accuracy.

Spam & Profanity Filters

Block unwanted calls and filter offensive language for a better experience.

Quick Responses

Use preset messages and keyboard-based responses for efficient communication.

Multilingual Support for All Indians

We understand India's linguistic diversity. Hearable supports 12 major Indian languages, making communication accessible to millions across the country.

Simply select your preferred language or switch between them as needed. We're also exploring Indian Sign Language (ISL) integration for users who rely on sign language.

Your Privacy & Security Matter

At Voxity, we believe that accessibility and privacy go hand in hand. Hearable is designed with strong privacy safeguards to protect your communication and personal data.

Local Storage Only

All transcripts and sensitive data are stored locally on your device, not on external servers.

DPDP Act Compliant

We fully comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act for all aspects of data handling.

Transparent Policies

Clear privacy policy and terms of service explaining how your data is used and stored.

Data Control

You have complete control over your data with easy options to delete or manage your information.
